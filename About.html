Cosmic Showdown

How to Play

Wizard's Jump - W

Move Wizard Left - A

Move Wizard Right - D

Wizard's Bonk - S

Witch's Jump - Up Arrow

Move Witch Left - Left Arrow

Move Witch Right - Right Arrow

Witch's Smack - Down Arrow
